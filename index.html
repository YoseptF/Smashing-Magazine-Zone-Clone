PERSON OF THE WEEK Leonardo Losoviz (@losoviz) is a freelance developer and consultant on an ongoing quest to integrate innovative paradigms (Serverless PHP, server-side components, GraphQL) into existing PHP frameworks (WordPress, Symfony, Laravel), and unifying all of them into a single mental model. He gives account of his progress on his blog leoloso.com, writing articles, and speaking at events across the globe. Leonardo also created PoP, an open-source project for building applications based on server-side components, and authored an improved version of GraphQL. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Leonardo!
